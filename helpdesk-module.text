# 2. Executive Summary

The Africa CDC Helpdesk & IT Service Management Platform shall be a centralized enterprise support system that enables Africa CDC staff to report, track, manage, and resolve technical and operational issues across all systems and departments.

The platform shall provide:

* Enterprise-grade ticket management
* AI-powered support automation
* Multi-channel support intake
* WhatsApp integration
* Microsoft Teams integration
* SLA management
* Multilingual support
* AI-assisted routing and summaries
* Knowledge base & FAQ management
* ISO-compliant auditing and security

---

# 3. Core Objectives

The system shall:

1. Centralize support operations
2. Improve support response times
3. Automate issue assignment
4. Improve support quality
5. Provide analytics and reporting
6. Support multilingual operations
7. Enable AI-enhanced workflows
8. Improve user satisfaction
9. Provide enterprise security and compliance
10. Support future scalability
